About Me

My name is Siyathokoza Malunga , I am a student at Durban University Of Technology. Currently doing my Deploma in ICT:Applications Development(DIIAD)

Embracing my roots in Port Shepstone , my educational journey has been a joyous adventure . From the early years at Sbonokuhle Primary to the shift to Denver Zoar Primary School, every chapter brought excitement . The transition to Mdlangaswa High School and later to a suburb near Scottburgh during Grade 9 felt like a thrilling exploration.

Completing my matric in 2022 was a culmination of enjoyable challenges, earning a bachelor's pass in Commerce. The enthusiasm continued in 2023 as I delved into the world of ICT App Development at DUT. Joining the IOT group sparked a genuine love for coding and robotics . Recently, I proudly took on the role of tutoring at the Lenovo Robogirl 2023 initiative, further fueling my passion for technology and education . My educational path is not just a series of steps but a delightful journey filled with passion, discovery , and a commitment to inspiring others.
